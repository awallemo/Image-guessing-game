How to play IMGuess:
SINGLE-PLAYER emoji_people
You are the guesser, and the goal of the game is to guess what the picture is. You start by seeing a part of the picture. After that you have 1 attempt to guess what is in the picture. If you can't guess correctly you will see an additional part of the picture, and you get 1 more guesse. It continues like this until you've guessed correctly.
TWO-PLAYER sports_kabaddi
One of you will be the Guesser and the other will be the Proposer. The Proposer will see the entire picture and chooses which parts of the picture the guesser will see. After the Proposer has chosen a part of the picture to show to the Guesser. The Guesser will guess what it is a picture of. The Guesser has 1 attempt to guess correctly before it goes back to the proposer. The Guesser can choose to pass the turn if they feel like it. If the Guesser couldnt guess correctly or passed their turn, the Proposer will chose another piece of the picture to show. This continues until the Guesser chooses correctly, or the entire picture has been shown.
